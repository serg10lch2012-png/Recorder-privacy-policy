Overview

SimpleAppTech (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile application Recorder handles user data. The app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information on external servers.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

The app does not collect:

  • Personal information
  • Usage analytics
  • Location data
  • Media or device identifiers

All audio files and content remain on your device only.

2. Permissions Used

RECORD_AUDIO

Allows audio recording when initiated by the user. No recordings are uploaded or shared.

FOREGROUND_SERVICE / FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MICROPHONE

Required to keep the recording service active during recording.

READ_MEDIA_AUDIO (Android 13 and below)

Allows access to existing audio files on your device. The app does not upload or analyze them.

WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 10 and below)

Used only to save recordings locally.

POST_NOTIFICATIONS

Used to display recording status notifications.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties. The app uses no advertising SDKs, cloud storage, or analytics tools.

4. Local Data Storage

All audio files remain on your device, under your full control. You may delete them at any time.

5. Security

Since all data is stored locally, its security relies on your device's settings and protection.

6. Children’s Privacy

The app is intended for users over 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

We may update this Privacy Policy. All updates will be available on this page.
